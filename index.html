TAHINA ANDRIANILANA - UX/UI DESIGNER

UX/UI Designer passionné par la compréhension des gens et des usages, je résous les problématiques à l'aide de démarches et méthodes centrées sur l'utilisateur. Avec un background en design d'ateliers collaboratifs (Design Sprints) et stratégie de marque, je suis à la recherche d'un poste en design d'expériences utilisateurs.

BIOGRAPHIE

I was born in 2001 in Hawaii where I was raised by ma grand-parens to become a professional surfer.

I have spent my entire adult life—and most of my (beleaguered) childhood—engrossed in internet business and the functions that drive it. As a somewhat atypical child—emigrating war-torn Yugoslavia for the United States at the age of ten—I found a virtual refuge in the online world, and quickly became enamored with design, development, and marketing on the (then new) medium.
